# Minute ⏱️

> Stay focused. Minute remembers your work.

Minute is a lightweight macOS application designed for people who constantly switch between tasks during the workday.

Instead of thinking about timers, spreadsheets, or daily reports, you simply work. Minute quietly keeps track of your activities so you can stay focused on what matters.

---

## Why Minute?

Minute started from a real problem.

Someone close to me had to manually track every activity throughout the workday in order to submit daily reports.

Switching between work and time tracking became frustrating and distracting.

Minute was created to remove that friction.

The goal isn't to help you manage your time.

The goal is to help you forget about it.
